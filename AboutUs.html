"Astronomy is useful because it raises us above ourselves; it is useful because it is grand; …. It shows us how small is man's body, how great his mind, since his intelligence can embrace the whole of this dazzling immensity, where his body is only an obscure point, and enjoy its silent harmony."
- Henri Poincare, Physicist
The Astronomer is a website meant to bring important news related to astronomy, we have news classified in three different categories, "Humans in Space", which contain news related to space expeditions or anything in space related to humans, "Earth", which contain news related to our planet and "Space Tech", with news related to astronomy and technology. This website is being developed for a college project with the objective of building a news website mainly with HTML, JS and CSS.
